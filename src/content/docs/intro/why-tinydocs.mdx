# Why TinyDocs

There are many documentation frameworks available, but TinyDocs stands out for its simplicity and minimal approach.

## Key Advantages

### Simple and Lightweight
TinyDocs doesn't force you into complex build processes or configurations. It's built on Astro's solid foundation and stays out of your way.

### Full Control
You own your content and presentation. No vendor lock-in, no complex theming systems - just clean, straightforward documentation.

### Fast Performance
With static site generation and minimal JavaScript, your documentation loads instantly. Users can find what they need quickly.

## When to Use TinyDocs

TinyDocs is perfect for:
- Technical documentation
- API references
- Product guides
- Knowledge bases
- Developer portals
